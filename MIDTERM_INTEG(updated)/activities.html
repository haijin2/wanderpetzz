Wander Pets' Activities

Wander Pets' activities significantly benefit the community of pets by providing essential resources such as lightweight, weatherproof foldable shelters, street feeding stations offering nutritious food and water, and donation drives for toys, treats, and bedding. Check our 3 activities below:

1

Foldable Shelter

2024

Imagine a lightweight, pop-up structure that unfolds to create a cozy haven for stray animals. This foldable shelter is a lifesaver for cats, dogs, or other critters braving the elements.

2

Street feeding stations

2024

These are set up in areas with stray animal populations, offering clean bowls of food and fresh waterThe heart of these stations are clean, metal bowls regularly filled with nutritious food appropriate for the local animal population.

3

Donation Drive

2024

Show some love to strays and donate items that go beyond the essentials! While food and water are crucial, you can bring some extra joy to their lives with toys, treats, and comfy bedding.
